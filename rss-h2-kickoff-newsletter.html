THE UPMARKET EFFICIENCY CASE
To reach our July target of $2.02M, the team needs to focus on deals with ≥15 Revenue Hub licenses — where the ASP delivers ~$1K of attributed Revenue Hub MRR per deal. At this threshold, the deal capacity constraint is no longer prohibitive, and the team gets a 3.4× lift per deal enabling far higher revenue impact.

Case in point: if every RSS specialist influences $10K MRR per deal rather than $3K, the team generates $40K MRR vs the initial $12K MRR baseline — a 3.3× improvement in total revenue contribution from the same headcount.

We are building a team incentive model to keep our focus on the larger prize — working the right deals so we are never spread thin, and can win.

Spread thin doesn't win. The data is unambiguous. Concentrating 4 specialists on the 34% of deals that meet the ≥15 license threshold is how this team scales toward a $35M ARR target.

HOW WE'RE ADDRESSING THESE CONSTRAINTS
We appreciate that this focus leaves hundreds of deals in the lower license tiers without direct specialist coverage. We're remedying this systematically — not ignoring it.
  • Headcount where available: Incremental MM rep additions and Commerce Specialists rolling into RSS will expand capacity for ≥15 deals as they come online
  • SST + SE org training: 5-week enablement track equips the broader SST field and SE swimlane to carry eligible deals with specialist-grade expertise
  • Enablement track for ineligible deals: Dedicated playbook and resources for deals below the 15-license threshold — ensuring GS can work them through effectively
  • Office hours + async content (H2): Ongoing sessions and content library for GS reps managing <15 license deals throughout the rest of the year — specialization expertise available without requiring a DSR

Specialization earns its keep by concentrating expertise where it drives the most revenue. The SST org is already making this move upmarket. RSS is aligned. And the deals that don't qualify for direct support will not be left without a path — they'll have tools, training, and access.
