Certification levels

Three levels. Each represents a defined capability threshold. L2 is the non-negotiable floor. RSS will be held to L3 as the operating standard for independent deal work.

Level Name What You Can Do Who It's For
L1 Harbor Tour Articulate the Revenue Hub value narrative. Deliver a top-level product walkthrough. Answer foundational questions without a script. CS and GS. Expected baseline awareness. Not sufficient for RSS or SST as specialists.
L2 End-to-End Seller Run the full Revenue Hub demo: quote → contract → billing → payments. Handle competitive objections (DealHub, SFDC CPQ, PandaDoc). Run discovery above the powerline. Take a deal from first meeting to signed contract independently. Minimum standard for all RSS and SST. No exceptions. GS reps enabling on CPQ attach target L2.
